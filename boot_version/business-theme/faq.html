FAQ

How does the ADHD coaching relationship work?

Coaching starts with a phone conversation to assess the clients particular challenges and determine the clients needs and desired outcomes. The coach & client meet weekly, scheduling a mutually agreed upon time that takes place either in person or by phone. The client brings the agenda of what he or she would like to focus on for each appointment while the coach supports the client by customizing strategies that help ensure the client finds success.

How do I know if I’m ready for ADHD/ADD coaching?

If you are ready to make positive changes in your life and you are looking to experience growth, be it personal, professional, or relational than coaching is right for you. Clients need to possess a commitment to be open to new ideas, strategies and approaches. They also need to be willing to implement the needed action steps to create the positive growth they desire.

How long does the coaching relationship last?

The length of the coaching relationship varies depending on the scope of the client’s needs and goals. I ask all clients to consider a 3 month commitment in order establish a working relationship and to promote noticeable change and progress. Many clients notice, the longer they are committed to the coaching process, the more results they achieve.
